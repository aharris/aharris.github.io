Traveler Connect is a website/app hybrid. It allows users to make calls out of the country for free over Wi-Fi as well as providing an endless amount of resources for local information, social media, and things to do. I designed Traveler Connect with a focus on friendly hospitality and usability. It's interface makes it easy for users to quickly find what is important to them on vacation.
